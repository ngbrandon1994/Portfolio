3. Planes Art Online 2D Unity Game

This is a 2D Unity Game that I which created for my Game Programming Class's Project. The requirement of the project was have an airplane shooting game, with a player controlled sprite and an AI sprite for an enemy controlled plane with automatically shooting bullets at the player. The other requirements were to have an animation, particle system, directions scene, highscore scene, consistent scores throughout the levels and to restart each level if the player lost the battle.

I reworked this project into called this game, "Planes Art Online", as I based the background and music off an anime and manga called "Sword Art Online". In Sword Art Online manga and anime, the character plays a Virtual reality game called Sword Art Online, and if they die in the game they die in real life. I took that concept and implement it in the game where if the player dies at a certain level, they will lose the game. For copyright purposes, I do not own the music used in this 2D Unity game as they are owned and created by Yuki Kajiura.
